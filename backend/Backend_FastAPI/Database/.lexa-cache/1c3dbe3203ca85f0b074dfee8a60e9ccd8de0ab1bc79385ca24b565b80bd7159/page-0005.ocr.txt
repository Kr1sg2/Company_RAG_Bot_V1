Gouges, Dents, Cracks: 
1. 
Find a fill stick that is a similar color to the wood. 
2. 
Firmly rub fill stick over area. 
 
3. 
Rub in with finger or paper towel. 
 
4. 
Remove extra fill stick material off of surrounding area. 
 
5. 
Spray lightly with gloss to match the shine.
