White Wicker 
Discolored area 
1.  Find a can of white gloss spray paint. 
2. Shake can vigorously for 5 – 10 seconds. 
3. Hold can 6” – 10” away from the area you are fixing. 
4. Press the nozzle and sweep across the area, release the nozzle 
before returning to the start position. 
5. Repeat step 4. Blending and painting lighter as you move away 
from the spot. 
6. Allow to dry and repeat if necessary.
