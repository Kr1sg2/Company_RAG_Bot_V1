Prolen 
1. Find a finish marker similar in color to the finish you are working on. 
2. Rub the marker over the area you are working on and the surrounding area to 
blend the color. 
3. Rub with your finger or paper towel if necessary to blend. 
 
 
DO NOT APPLY SPRAY PAINT 
OR BLUSH TO PROLENE. 
IT WILL MAKE IT STICKY
