Viewing the Digital Catalog in NetSuite 
Shortcut:  NetSuite/Vendor Record/Pricelists and Information Tab/Point of Sale Subtab 
Traditionaly, manufacturer catalogs were printed every year and hard copies were distributed.  
Catalogs are now digital and can be quickly searched using by the computer instead of flipping 
page by page.   
 
Search Short Cut:  <CTRL><F>, type what you are looking for in the search box. 
 
 
 
Using the Item Lookup Shortcut 
When you are learning the system start with the Item Lookup portal on the NetSuite home 
screen.
