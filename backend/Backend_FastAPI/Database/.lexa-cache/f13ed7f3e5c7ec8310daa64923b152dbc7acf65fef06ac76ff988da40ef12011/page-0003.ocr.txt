3.18 Customer Communication In NetSuite 
6/9/2019 
How Do I See What Information Has Been Sent? 
1. From the NetSuite home screen use Guest Lookup to find the guest record. 
 
2. Open the guest record in View mode 
3. On the “Transactions” or “Opportunity” tab locate the record you are referencing 
 
4. View the communication record for the transaction by going to the “Communication” subtab on 
the Sales Order or Quote and clicking 
. 
 
5. A pop up will allow you to view the details of the communication.
