Collection Easels & Price Tags 
Collection Signs & Price Tags can be found on any item record. 
1. Look up an item using the item look up in the Navigation Shortcut on your dashboard. 
 
2. Use the filters to find the item you are looking for 
3. On the item record select the Pricing Tab and then the Point of Purchase Files 
a. You can find 
i. Collection Tear Sheets 
ii. Catalog Pages 
iii. Price Tags 
iv. Floor Easels
