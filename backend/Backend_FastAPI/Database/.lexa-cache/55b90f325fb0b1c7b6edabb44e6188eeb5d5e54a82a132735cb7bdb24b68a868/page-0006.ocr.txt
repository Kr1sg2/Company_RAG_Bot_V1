Set Items Purchased To Images 
To make reviewing guests purchases visual and easy.  This setting is “sticky” so once you set the filter to 
Images it will stay this way on all other customer records till you change it.   
 
1. From any customer record go to the Sales tab 
2. On the sales tab click the Items Purchased subtab 
3. Change the View in the drop down menu to “Sublist | Customer Items with Images
