5.5 Gift Certificates 
3/2/2022 
Selling a Gift Certificate 
The gift certificate should be sold under the account of the person paying for the gift certificate.  During 
the selling process, you can assign the gift certificate to a recipient.   
1. In NetSuite, find the customer account for the person buying the gift certificate.  If they do not 
have a customer record create one. 
2. Write a 
sale like you would for regular product 
3. Complete “1. Primary Information” 
4. Enter "Gift Certificate" as a line item 
5. Fill out the value of the gift card in the amount field. 
 
6. On the right side of the Gift Certificate line item, click in the Gift Certificate box.
