Bulk SKU Tag Example 
 
1. Item Number   A unique combination of letters and number that give an item an identity 
2. Price  The everyday selling price of an item 
The item number printed on a bulk tag is not the complete item number. In this example the 
complete item number is STOT-1500,250.  (Vendor: Stotter Item: 1500 Color: 250)
