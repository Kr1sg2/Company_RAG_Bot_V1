 
 
-Touch-Up Loomed Wicker- 
 
Supplies Needed 
1. Furniture Touch-Up Marker of Similar tone to Wicker 
2. Paper Towel 
3. Blush Furniture Spray Paint 
 
 
1. Find a marker similar in color to the finish you are working on 
 
2. Rub the marker over the area you are working on and the surrounding area to blend the color. 
 
3. Rub with your finger or paper towel if necessary to blend 
 
4. Apply a light coat of blush to blend the shine
