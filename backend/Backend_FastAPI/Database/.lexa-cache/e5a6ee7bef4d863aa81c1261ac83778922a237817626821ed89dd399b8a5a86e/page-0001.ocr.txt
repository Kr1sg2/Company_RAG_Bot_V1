7.12.4 Embroidered Toss Pillows 
8/23/2021 
 
Embroidered Toss Pillows 
 
Embroidery is currently available on any of the Toss Pillow Styles listed on the next page. You can 
choose from any fabric and any Embroidery Design found in the configurator.   
 
Special Notes: 
▪ 
Normal Special Order lead time applies. 
▪ 
Custom Sayings, Designs, Text, etc. are not available at this time on retail sales.  
▪ 
Custom logos are possible on contract and designer sales with minimum order. Graphic must 
be provided by the customer. Pricing is based off of size and complexity of the design.  
▪ 
Pillows, Umbrellas and some sling fabrics can be embroidered. Please contact Tim for pricing 
and minimum quantity specifications. 
▪ 
All embroidery will be sewn in the thread colors pictured in the catalog unless otherwise 
noted on the     image. 
▪ 
Custom thread colors are not available at this time. 
▪ 
All embroidery designs will be scaled to best fit to the toss pillow size as seen fit by our 
professional seamstress.
