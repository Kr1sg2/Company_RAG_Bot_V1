Install the New Spring Plates 
1. Use the original hardware to install the new spring plates.  Take care to put the washers back in 
place to prevent the new spring plate from cracking during use. 
2. Tighten the spring plates with the two wrenches.  Take care not to over tighten and crack the new 
spring plate
