0.7 Dress Code Policy 
1/11/2019 
 
Dress Code Policy 
Dress for Success! 
Store team members are expected to maintain a well-groomed and professional appearance.  They may 
not wear jeans, shorts, sneakers, or flip flops.  A name tag must be worn at all times.  Extreme accessories 
are not permitted.  Men’s shirts must be collared.  Store teams are permitted to wear Leader's Casual 
Furniture® polo shirts if they choose to purchase them.
