 
 
-Touch-Up Resin Outdoor Wicker- 
 
Supplies Needed 
1. Furniture Touch-Up Marker of Similar tone to Wicker 
2. Paper Towel 
 
 
1. Find a furniture touch-up finish marker similar in color to the finish you are working on 
2. Rub the marker over the area you are working on and the surrounding area to blend the 
color. 
3. Rub with your finger or paper towel if necessary to blend 
 
 
 
 
 
DO NOT APPLY SPRAY PAINT OR BLUSH. 
IT WILL MAKE THE PROLEN STICKY
