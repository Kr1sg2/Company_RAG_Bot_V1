Installing Replacement Spring Plates 
11/28/2022 
 
Install the new spring plates 
1. Use the original hardware to install the new spring plates.  Take care to put the washers back in place to 
prevent the new spring plate from cracking during use. 
2. Tighten the spring plates with the two wrenches.  Take care not to over tighten and crack the new spring 
plate
