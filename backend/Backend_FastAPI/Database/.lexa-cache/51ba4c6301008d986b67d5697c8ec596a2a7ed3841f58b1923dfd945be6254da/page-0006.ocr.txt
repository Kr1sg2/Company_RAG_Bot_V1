c. Carefully lift and stand the cushion on end while applying light pressure to the tool to keep it in the 
cushion.  Lightly add pressure to the tool to guide it out the existing hole on the other side of the 
cushion.  Be careful not to press to hard and puncture the fabric. 
d. Once the hole is located and the button tool is sticking out push down firmly on the cushion to expos the 
notch in the end of the tool. Insert the string for the button you prepared into the notch and allow the 
cushion to re expand.  
 
e. Pull the button tool out the other side of the cushion and keep tension the button.
