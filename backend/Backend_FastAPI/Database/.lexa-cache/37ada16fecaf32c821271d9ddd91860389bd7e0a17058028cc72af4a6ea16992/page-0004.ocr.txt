Clearing History 
If a payment fails after the PAX machine batches out, the history may have not been cleared.  To help 
prevent payments from failing it is important that a PAX device clear its history every night.  Follow 
these steps to set the machine up to clear its history every night. 
1. On the PAX device, click on the circle at the bottom of the device to go to the app screen 
2. Select the PayGuardian App 
 
3. Click on the three dot ellipsis in the top right of the screen 
 
4. Click “Toggle Clear History”
