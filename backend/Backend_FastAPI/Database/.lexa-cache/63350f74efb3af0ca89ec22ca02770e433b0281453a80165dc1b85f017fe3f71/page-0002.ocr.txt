The Leaders’ team uses the following consistent naming conventions 
when posting pictures to make them referenceable without unwanted 
results.   
 
Brand 
Leaders uses the names that the manufacturers themselves use when they post.  Some of these names 
may have additional words than what you are familiar with.  Use the table below as a reference. 
 
“#” – “Manufacturer Name” 
Example:  #Troptione
