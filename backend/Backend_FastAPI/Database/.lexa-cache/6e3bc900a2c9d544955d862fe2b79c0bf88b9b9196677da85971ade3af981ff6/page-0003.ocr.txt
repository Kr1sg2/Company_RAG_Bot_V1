Accessing Time Clock Plus 
Logging in is done a little differently depending on the area you participate at Leaders.  If you use NetSuite throughout 
the day to complete your responsibilities, you will click a short cut in NetSuite.  If you do not use NetSuite you will use 
the Clock In Clock out computer near the warehouse office.   
NetSuite Shortcut 
1. Log Into NetSuite 
2. From your Favorites drop down menu select “Time Clock” 
 
3. Enter your employee ID Number
