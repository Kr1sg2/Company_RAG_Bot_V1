2.92.3 How to use Room Planner 
6/21/2018 
 
How: does the Room Planner work? 
Once you’ve selected “Room Planner” from the Main Menu, the following screen will appear.  
Simply click on the bottom right that says, “Click Here to Open Room Planner.” 
 
Template vs. Create Your Own: 
At the next screen you have two options.  Option 1,  use a pre-programmed template, or Option 
2, start the space from scratch and create your own plan. 
 
 The Pre-Programmed templates are available for the following room sizes: 
 Bedroom, 22’ x 15’
