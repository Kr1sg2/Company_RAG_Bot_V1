Scratches to bare wood: 
Please use the following Mohawk touch up supplies.  If you need help acquiring Mohawk 
supplies, please contact the Solutions Center at solutions.center@leadersfurniture.com 
Pecan Glaze 
Maple brown or universal cherry marker.  Fill stick 230-0408 or similar color as the wood 
Whitewash 
Whitewash marker (blend with finger or paper towel if too light) 
Natural 
Clearcoat spray 
1. 
Apply marker to the affected area and surrounding area to blend. 
2. 
If the marker blots up, rub with your finger to blend.  You may have to let it dry 
for 30 seconds and apply another coat to get it darker. 
3. 
If the shine on the finish is not consistent, apply blush spray to the touched-up 
location and spray surrounding areas to blend.
