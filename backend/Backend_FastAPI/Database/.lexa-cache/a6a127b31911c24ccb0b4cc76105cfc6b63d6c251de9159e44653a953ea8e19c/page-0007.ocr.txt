Minor Touch-Up and Repair 
Please contact your local store or the Solutions Center if you need help acquiring any of the Mohawk 
touch-up products. 
Minor Scratches: 
Rub with lemon oil rag or apply blush spray. 
Surface paint from another item: 
1. Pour acetone on a rag and allow to dry for 5 seconds. 
2. Lightly wipe the area where you want to remove the paint. 
Or 
3. Sand the area lightly with steel wool. 
4. Spray a light coat of blush to bring back the shine.
