7.12.1 Central Cushion Rush 
7/15/2022 
Do we have the fabric in stock? 
We can’t rush an order if we don’t have the fabric to make it.  Please do not forget this critical step. 
1. In NetSuite click on the Products Tab 
 
2. The first window will show you a fabric list and the number of rolls available for each fabric.  You 
can search for a specific fabric by typing the fabric number in the search box in the window.
