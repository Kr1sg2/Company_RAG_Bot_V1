2.92.4 Rugs Monthly Focus 
6/21/2018 
 
 
 Furniture along the wall- Cover halfway, by aiming to have the front two legs of all furniture on the rug.   
 
 Small Living Area- Center Rug.  Smaller rugs can be used just under the cocktail table, or to define the specific 
seating area. 
                      
  
 Dining Room- Your table and chairs should rest on the rug. 24“or so is needed beyond each side of the table to 
avoid chairs going off the rug when pushed back.
