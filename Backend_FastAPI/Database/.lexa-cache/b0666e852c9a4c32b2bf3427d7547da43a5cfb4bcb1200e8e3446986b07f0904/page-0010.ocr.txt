File a Warranty Claim / Order Parts 
We are here to help you after the sale.  To help us process your claim as fast as possible please 
complete the following steps. 
Step 1: Take a picture of each item with concern in a format that you will be able to attach to an 
e-mail. 
1. Take a picture of the entire item with a concern 
2. Take a close-up picture of the concern 
3. Repeat for every item with a concern 
 
Step 2:  Send an e-mail to solutions.center@leadersfurniture.com and include the following 
✓ Your name 
✓ Phone number 
✓ The number of items with which you have a concern 
✓ A description of each concern 
✓ A picture of each item of concern and a close-up photo of each concern 
 
Step 3:  One of our Solutions Center Representatives will get back to you within 24 hours.  If you 
do not hear back for any reason within 24 hours, please contact us toll-free at 
(877) 538-5783.
